01 Who We Are

Span Healthcare Limited ("Span", "we", "us", or "our") is a technology platform registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria. We operate a Sharia-compliant health savings platform combining a dedicated health wallet, halal investment participation through a Wakala-Mudarabah structure, telemedicine services, and access to a network of verified healthcare providers.

Our registered address is No. 11B, Sambo Road, City Centre, Kaduna, Nigeria. For all privacy-related matters, you may contact us at info@span-healthcare.com.

Span Healthcare processes personal data as a Data Controller under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA 2023) and the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR).

02 Information We Collect

Identity and KYC Information

  • Full legal name, date of birth, gender, and marital status
  • National Identification Number (NIN) and Bank Verification Number (BVN)
  • Government-issued ID type and number
  • Passport photograph

Contact Information

  • Phone number, WhatsApp number, and email address
  • Residential address, state, local government area, and landmark

Health Information

  • Blood group and genotype
  • Chronic conditions and pre-existing medical history (as disclosed by you)
  • Next of kin details
  • Telemedicine consultation records and prescriptions

Financial Information

  • Deposit and transaction history within your health wallet
  • Investment return credits and Mudarabah pool participation records
  • Payment processing data handled by our licensed PSP partner

Technical Information

  • Device type, browser, IP address, and operating system
  • Pages visited, session duration, and interaction data
  • Referral source and referral codes used during registration

03 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To verify your identity and process your account application in compliance with KYC requirements
  • To operate and maintain your health savings wallet
  • To facilitate telemedicine consultations and drug delivery services
  • To process payments and manage your financial transactions
  • To calculate and credit Mudarabah investment returns to your wallet
  • To connect you with verified healthcare providers in our network
  • To send you service notifications, transaction confirmations, and health reminders
  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations under Nigerian law
  • To improve our platform, services, and user experience
  • To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, security threats, and abuse

We do not use your health information for marketing purposes. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your financial and health data are strictly purpose-restricted to the delivery of Span Healthcare services.

04 How We Share Your Information

We share your information only where necessary and with the following categories of recipients:

Service Partners

  • Konooz Investment — our Mudarabah investment partner receives anonymised pool participation data necessary to manage the halal investment fund
  • Licensed Payment Service Provider — processes transactions on our behalf under CBN regulation
  • MDCN-registered doctors — receive your health information necessary to conduct telemedicine consultations
  • Verified healthcare providers — receive treatment authorisation and payment confirmation from Span

Technology Partners

  • Supabase — our cloud database provider, for secure data storage
  • Vercel — our hosting and deployment platform
  • Agora — video and audio infrastructure for telemedicine consultations

Regulatory and Legal Disclosure

  • We may disclose your information to regulators, law enforcement, or courts where required by Nigerian law or a valid legal order
  • We share data with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) in line with our registration and compliance obligations

05 Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure hosted within or accessible from the European Union via Supabase. We are actively working to resolve data residency to Nigeria in compliance with Section 41 of the NDPA 2023 and will update this policy when that transition is complete.

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • Row-level security (RLS) on all database tables — your data is accessible only to you and authorised Span administrators
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Role-based access control for internal staff
  • Regular security reviews and access audits

No system is 100% secure. While we take all reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet. We will notify affected users and the NDPC promptly in the event of a data breach as required by the NDPA 2023.

06 Your Rights Under the NDPA 2023

As a data subject under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — you may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — you may request deletion of your data, subject to our legal retention obligations
  • Right to restriction — you may request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to data portability — you may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — you may object to processing of your data for marketing or profiling purposes
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@span-healthcare.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by the NDPA 2023. If you are unsatisfied with our response, you may file a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission at ndpc.gov.ng.

07 Cookies and Tracking

Our marketing website uses essential cookies to maintain session state and improve performance. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels on our platform.

  • Essential cookies — required for the platform to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — used to understand how users interact with our platform in aggregate, anonymised form

You may disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings. This will not affect your ability to use the Span Healthcare platform.

08 Children's Privacy

Span Healthcare accounts may include dependent coverage for children under 18 as part of a family plan managed by an adult account holder. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children under 18 without verifiable parental or guardian consent.

If you believe a child's data has been submitted without appropriate consent, contact us immediately at info@span-healthcare.com and we will act promptly to review and delete the data.

09 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal obligations, or regulatory requirements. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last amended" date at the top of this document
  • Notify registered users via email or in-app notification at least 14 days before the changes take effect
  • Require re-acknowledgement of the updated policy where required by law

Your continued use of Span Healthcare services after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
